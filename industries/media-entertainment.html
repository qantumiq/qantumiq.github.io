Industry Challenges

The pressures you face

The media and entertainment landscape has undergone its most disruptive decade in history. Streaming saturation, AI-generated content, rights complexity, and the collapse of third-party data have converged simultaneously.

Subscriber Growth Saturation

Global streaming subscription growth has plateaued. With consumers subscribing to an average of 4.2 platforms, customer acquisition costs have tripled since 2020 and churn has become the primary business metric.

Generative AI Content Disruption

AI-generated scripts, synthetic voice, automated localization, and deepfake video are simultaneously threatening creative roles and unlocking 10× content scale — creating both existential risk and opportunity.

Rights & IP Complexity

Managing content rights across global markets, AVOD/SVOD/TVOD windows, AI training datasets, and derivative works is increasingly complex — and the regulatory environment is evolving rapidly.

Cookieless Advertising Transition

Third-party cookie deprecation is forcing a complete rebuild of targeting, measurement, and attribution infrastructure — while Privacy Sandbox, clean rooms, and contextual intelligence require significant investment.

Linear-to-Digital Migration Pressure

Cord-cutting is accelerating, pay-TV subscribers continue their structural decline, and legacy broadcast networks face an existential pressure to transition revenue models and audiences to digital platforms.
