Water Sustainability

Water challenges

Global Water Scarcity

2.3 billion people live in water-stressed regions. Climate change, population growth, and agricultural demand are accelerating the crisis — the World Bank projects a 40% supply-demand gap by 2030.

Aging Water Infrastructure

In developed nations, water and wastewater systems lose 20–30% of treated water to leaks. Utilities face trillion-dollar deferred maintenance backlogs and need intelligent asset management.

Water Quality & Contamination

PFAS, microplastics, pharmaceutical residues, and industrial pollutants require advanced treatment technologies and real-time monitoring at scales not yet deployed.

Climate-Driven Flood & Drought

Extreme weather events are straining stormwater systems, threatening agricultural water supply, and causing billions in flood damage — demanding AI-powered prediction and response.
