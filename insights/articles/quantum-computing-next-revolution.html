Quantum Computing: The Next Revolution

QantumIQ Research · April 2025 · 11 min read

The Quantum Opportunity

Quantum computing represents the most fundamental shift in computation since the transistor. By harnessing superposition and entanglement, quantum computers solve problems intractable for classical supercomputers.

Hardware Landscape

Multiple approaches compete: superconducting qubits (IBM, Google) exceeding 1,000 qubits; trapped ions (IonQ, Quantinuum) with superior connectivity; photonic systems (PsiQuantum) promising room-temperature operation.

Industry Applications

Near-term applications emerge in pharmaceutical simulation, financial optimization, logistics, and materials science. Quantum computers naturally simulate quantum systems — discovering new chemistries and materials.

Post-Quantum Security

Quantum computers could break RSA and ECC protecting digital communications. NIST has finalized standards — begin migration planning now.

Building Readiness

Assess computational challenges, build quantum literacy, invest in data infrastructure for hybrid workflows. Strong classical foundations position you best.
