AI-Driven CMC Optimization in Pharma

QantumIQ Research · February 2025 · 9 min read

The CMC Challenge

CMC accounts for 30-40% of drug development costs. Much knowledge remains tacit, creating enormous AI optimization opportunity.

ML in Formulation

Predictive models screen excipient combinations, predict stability, optimize processing. Bayesian optimization efficiently explores vast design spaces.

Process Analytical Technology

ML extracts signals from NIR, Raman, and particle analyzers predicting quality in real time. Anomaly detection identifies deviations before quality impact.

Regulatory Intelligence

NLP analyzes submissions identifying potential queries before reviewers raise them.

Scale-Up

AI models capturing cross-scale relationships reduce technology transfer risk. Digital twins enable virtual experimentation.
