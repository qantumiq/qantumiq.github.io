Last updated: January 1, 2025

QantumIQ Consulting ("QantumIQ," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you complete a consultation request form, subscribe to our newsletters, register for events, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title.

We also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including IP addresses, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages viewed. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website.

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries and consultation requests, provide and improve our services, send you marketing communications (with your consent where required), conduct research and analytics, comply with legal obligations, and protect the security of our services.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with our affiliated offices and subsidiaries, trusted service providers who assist in our operations, professional advisors, and as required by law. Data Security

QantumIQ implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Given our expertise in quantum-safe security, we apply leading-edge cryptographic standards to protect data in transit and at rest.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@qantumiq.com. Residents of the European Economic Area, UK, California, and other jurisdictions have additional rights under applicable data protection laws.

International Transfers

QantumIQ operates globally with offices in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and India.
